\section{Research Interests}




\begin{onecolentry}
    My planned research aims to leverage over two decates of professional experience in the IT industry to explore historical perspectives on the Pascal programming language.
    I am interested in investigating Pascal's role in response to the complexities of the Algol-68 specification, its influence on programming education and home computing, and the business history of the Borland software corporation through its Pascal implementation.
\end{onecolentry}
