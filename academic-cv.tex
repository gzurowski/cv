\section{Research Interests}




\begin{onecolentry}
    Planned research aims to leverage over two decades of professional experience in the IT industry to explore historical perspectives on the Pascal programming language.
    Key interests include investigating Pascal's role in response to the complexities of the ALGOL 68 specification, its influence on programming education and home computing, and the business history of the Borland software corporation through its Pascal implementation.
\end{onecolentry}
